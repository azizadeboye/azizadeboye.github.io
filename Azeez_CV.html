Azeez Adeboye

BIO-DATA

Azeez Adeboye is a Lecturer at the department of Statistics, University of Fort Hare, Alice, 5700, Eastern Cape, South Africa. He received his PhD (Biostatistics) at the University of Fort Hare on joint modelling of Longitudinal mixed models and survival models with weighted function apply to biomedical and clinical trial data. He has gained expertise in various statistical topics with research interests, including Bayesian approaches, Longitudinal analysis, statistical modelling, survival analysis, and mixed/random-effects models. His research as led to several papers published in high quality international and national journals. During his study, Dr Azeez gained clinical trials and medical studies experiences as well as bioinformatics training at the University of Cape Town, South Africa. Dr Azeez is an experienced R user with many functions derived and currently working on an R package development. Besides, he has an excellent working knowledge of the following software: SAS, JAGS, STAN, STATA, Julia, Python, and SPSS e.t.c.
